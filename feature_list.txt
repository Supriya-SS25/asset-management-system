CLIENT REQUIREMENTS UNDERSTANDING
================================================================================

The client requires a software solution for their company's asset management system
with the core functionality to track which item is with which employee. This is a
fundamental asset tracking need that involves:

- Maintaining an inventory of company assets
- Tracking asset assignments to employees
- Monitoring asset location and status
- Ensuring accountability for company property

The system needs to be intuitive, reliable, and provide clear visibility into
asset distribution within the organization.

================================================================================
CREATIVE FREEDOMS TAKEN
================================================================================

1. Product Name: "AssetTrack Pro" - Professional and descriptive name
2. Technology Stack: Modern web-based solution for accessibility
3. User Interface: Clean, responsive design for desktop and mobile
4. Scope Expansion: Included additional features beyond basic tracking
5. Asset Categories: Added categorization for better organization
6. Reporting System: Included analytics and reporting capabilities
7. Notification System: Added alerts for overdue assets
8. User Roles: Implemented role-based access control

================================================================================
ASSUMPTIONS MADE ABOUT CLIENT REQUIREMENTS
================================================================================

1. The client has basic computer literacy and needs a user-friendly interface
2. Assets include IT equipment, furniture, tools, and other company property
3. Employee information includes basic details like name, department, and contact
4. Assets have unique identifiers (serial numbers, asset tags)
5. The system needs to support multiple administrators/managers
6. Historical tracking of asset assignments is important
7. The client may need to generate reports for audits
8. Mobile access would be beneficial for on-the-go scanning/check-in
9. Integration with existing HR systems might be needed later
10. The company size is small to medium (10-500 employees)

================================================================================
FEATURE LIST
================================================================================

1. User Authentication & Management
   - Secure login system with role-based access
   - Admin, Manager, and Employee roles
   - User profile management
   - Password reset functionality

2. Asset Management
   - Add, edit, and delete assets
   - Asset categorization (IT Equipment, Furniture, Tools, etc.)
   - Asset details including serial numbers, purchase date, warranty
   - Asset status tracking (Available, Assigned, Maintenance, Retired)
   - Barcode/QR code generation for assets

3. Employee Management
   - Employee database with basic information
   - Department and role assignment
   - Contact information management
   - Employee asset history

4. Asset Assignment & Tracking
   - Assign assets to employees
   - Check-in/check-out functionality
   - Due date tracking for assignments
   - Transfer assets between employees
   - Bulk assignment capabilities

5. Reporting & Analytics
   - Asset inventory reports
   - Employee asset summary
   - Overdue asset reports
   - Asset utilization analytics
   - Export reports to PDF/Excel

6. Notification System
   - Email alerts for overdue assets
   - Maintenance reminders
   - Low stock notifications for consumables
   - System notifications for important events

7. Search & Filter
   - Advanced search by asset name, serial number, employee
   - Filter by category, status, department
   - Real-time search results
   - Saved search preferences

8. Audit Trail
   - Complete history of asset movements
   - User activity logs
   - Change tracking for asset details
   - Timestamp for all transactions

9. Dashboard
   - Overview of key metrics
   - Recent activities feed
   - Quick action buttons
   - Asset status summary charts

10. Mobile Responsiveness
    - Responsive design for tablets and phones
    - Touch-friendly interface
    - Mobile-optimized scanning capabilities

8. Future Growth

   - Are there any additional features you might need in the future?
   - How do you see your asset management needs evolving?
